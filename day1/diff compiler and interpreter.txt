QUESTION: DIFFERENCE BETWEEN INTERPRETER AND COMPILER.

ANSWER:

COMPILER:

-Compiler translates the complete source program in a single run.
-It consumes less time.
-It is more efficient.
-Compilers are large in size.
-Error localization is difficult.
-Used by c,c++

INTERPRETER:

-An interpreter translates the source program line by line.
-It consumes more time tham compiler.
-Less efficient.
-Interpreters are smaller in size.
-Error localization in easier.
-Used by Python,Java
